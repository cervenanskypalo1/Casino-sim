European roulette, 37 pockets (single zero). The wheel is a genuinely fair, unweighted random draw over all 37 pockets every spin — exactly like a physical wheel — there is no configurable probability table here, unlike the slot. The entire house edge comes from the payout odds: every standard bet covers some fraction of the 37 pockets but pays slightly less than true odds, which is why every bet type below computes to exactly the same 36/37 = 97.30% RTP.

Bet Type Risk Profile

This is not a search for a "better" bet. Every roulette bet has identical 36/37 = 97.30% theoretical RTP — that's inherent to a fair wheel. What genuinely differs is volatility: fewer numbers covered means rarer, bigger wins and higher bust risk for the exact same expected value. This compares that risk profile across bet-coverage categories at equal stake (same Starting Balance / Spins / Sessions settings above).
